ABOUT US



COMPASS SCHOOL OF CIVILSERVICE has been set up to ignite the young minds to explore the world of beaurocracy. We nurture ambitious and energetic Indian youth to serve the country with power and pride. Compass School of Civil Services helps you to develop the leadership qualities hidden in you and crack the most prestigious examination in the country to imprint your signature in the history of India. Our Flagship Classroom course is a comprehensive 15 month full time UPSC course where we guide civil service aspirants from the scratch and teach them every aspect of the Civil Services Examination. This course consists of Classroom lectures, Study Materials and Test Series
